THE SORTING HAT:

At Hogwarts it magically determines which of the four school Houses each new student belongs most to. The four Houses are Gryffindor, Hufflepuff, Ravenclaw, and Slytherin.The Sorting Hat originally belonged to Godric Gryffindor, one of the four founders of Hogwarts. It normally will stay in the Headmaster's office until it is needed.
