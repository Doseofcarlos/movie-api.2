What do the houses mean?

Slytherin-

Tend to be ambitious, shrewd, cunning, strong leaders, and achievement-oriented. They also have highly developed senses of self-preservation.This means that Slytherins tend to hesitate before acting, so as to weigh all possible outcomes before deciding exactly what should be done.

Hufflepuff

Hard-working, friendly, loyal, honest and rather impartial. It may be that due to their values, Hufflepuffs are not as competitive as the other houses, and are more modest about their accomplishments.

Ravenclaw-

Possess the traits of cleverness, wisdom, wit, intellectual ability and creativity.Ravenclaws are so competitive when it comes to academic success that they are known to back stab each other, and likely other students, in order to get top marks. Many well-known inventors and innovators have come from Ravenclaw.

Gryffindor-

Emphasises the traits of courage as well as "daring, nerve, and chivalry,"and thus its members are generally regarded as brave, though sometimes to the point of recklessness.
